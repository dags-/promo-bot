#Promotion

Submit an application to add your server or youtube/twitch channel to the official #promotion channel on the Conquest Reforged discord.

#promotion is a free service intended to provide relevant server & social-media suggestions to the ConquestReforged community. While the service aims to provide fair exposure for all it's accepted submissions, this cannot be guaranteed. By using this service you agree that the ConquestReforged team bears no responsibility to accept, relay, or maintain any submission to the service, and may terminate the service itself at it's own discretion.
